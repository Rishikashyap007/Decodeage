FAQ

How does the tiered subscription plan work?

The longer you subscribe, the more savings you'll receive from our tiered subscription plan. Discounts grow to 25% after the sixth month and to a whopping 40% after a year, with a 10% discount initially applied.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

You will forfeit your current discount level if you decide to terminate your subscription. When you resubscribe, the 10% discount will start at the Starter level.

What is the Elite level?

Customers that have been with us for a year or longer are eligible for the Elite level. At this level, you receive a massive 40% off. You will save 25% on your next orders if you stay subscribed for more than a year.

Can I skip a month in my subscription?

You must continue your subscription in order to keep your discount level. If you were to skip a month, it would be seen as a cancellation, and when you resumed your subscription, you would be back at the Starter level.

When will I receive my products each month?

Monthly product dispatch will occur according to the date of your initial order. Every time your next dispatch is scheduled, we'll send you an email to let you know it's on its way.
