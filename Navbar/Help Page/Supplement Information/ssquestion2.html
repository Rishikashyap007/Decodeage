Can you give me spermidine? Recognising Dosage and Directions

If you're considering incorporating spermidine supplements into your routine, it's essential to determine an appropriate dosage. Here are brief guidelines to help you make an informed decision:

Is It Safe to Supplement With Spermidine?

Indeed, spermidine is a naturally occurring substance in the human body. Taking supplements containing spermidine may have favourable effects on various aspects of health, including ageing in a healthy manner. However, it's best to speak with a healthcare provider before incorporating it into your routine. Additionally, its usage should be avoided by those who are nursing or pregnant.

Can Spermidine Be Taken?

For most people, spermidine is usually regarded as safe. It is a substance that is found naturally in the body and is essential to autophagy. But like any supplement, there are a few things to keep in mind:

1. Health Conditions:

Before beginning Spermidine, it is imperative that you speak with a healthcare professional if you use medication or have any underlying medical conditions.

2. Pregnancy and Breastfeeding:

Little is known about the effects of spermidine on these processes. Supplementing with spermidine should be avoided at these times.

3. Age Factor:

Spermidine's anti-aging properties are the main focus of research. Before using, young people, especially those under the age of 18, should speak with a healthcare professional.

Suggested Dosage and Time:

The ideal dosage for adults is 10 to 15 mg of spermidine per day. (R)

How much spermidine is recommended for adults between the ages of 18 and 50? It is advised that people between the ages of 18 and 50 take one capsule or 10 mg of Decode Age Spermidine each day. One capsule of Decode Age Spermidine every day is the recommended beginning point. What dosage of spermidine is recommended for persons over the age of fifty? A greater dosage of 20 mg or two capsules of Decode Age Spermidine per day may be more advantageous when people approach 50. Starting with one capsule and working your way up to two is the suggested regimen.

When is the ideal time to use supplements containing spermidine?

One capsule should be taken every morning and evening, ideally right after eating. More specifically, people over 50 should take two capsules daily, while those between the ages of 18 and 50 should take one capsule in the morning.
