'beta' Nicotinamide Mononucleotide: Why is it used?

A nucleotide known as nicotinamide mononucleotide, or NMN, is commonly required for the metabolism of cellular energy. Moreover, it directly precedes nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide, or NAD+, which is involved in controlling cell function. The two stereo isomers of NMN are Œ±-type and Œ≤-type. It is noteworthy that the only NMN with biological action is the Œ≤-type. This indicates that this particular isomer is required for NMN supplements to be effective. Because Œ≤-NMN actively contributes to the conversion process to NAD+, it is necessary in supplements meant to increase the body's levels of NAD+. As we age, increased mitochondrial energy generation is beneficial and is dependent on higher NAD+ levels. Elevated NAD+ levels also contribute to the preservation and enhancement of mitochondrial health and function, which boosts metabolism and increases fat burning. Finally, NAD+'s role as a co-factor that initiates DNA repair is linked to its anti-aging properties. For extended periods of time, cellular health depends on the repair of damaged DNA. Therefore, the Œ≤-type NMN contributes to increased NAD+ levels, which enhance mitochondrial health, DNA repair, and energy generation. Make sure the NMN supplement you choose solely contains the Œ≤-type NMN by carefully reading the ingredients list.

Distinctions Among NMN Isomers

  • Œ±-NMN: Despite having a similar structure, Œ±-NMN is devoid of the biological activity required for the production of NAD+. Consequently, it does not support the same cellular processes as Œ≤-NMN.

  • Œ≤-NMN:The active isomer that directly helps to raising NAD+ levels is called Œ≤-NMN. Supplements that include Œ≤-NMN are more efficient at promoting general health, energy production, and cellular processes.

Why Decode Age Supplements Prefer Œ≤-NMN

  • Effectiveness: NAD+ is essential for cellular energy and general health, and Œ≤-NMN is effective in raising NAD+ levels.

  • Targeted Action: Œ≤-NMN supports NAD+-dependent cellular processes more effectively since it is the active form.

In conclusion ...

although NMN is a useful molecule overall, the Œ≤-NMN isomer is the more desirable option when it comes to NMN supplementation because it is biologically active and efficient in NAD+ production. In addition to supporting healthy NAD+ synthesis and helping to restore normal NAD+ levels, NMN may help extend life and fend off age-related alterations. Still, it's crucial to consult a physician prior to beginning NMN supplementation.
