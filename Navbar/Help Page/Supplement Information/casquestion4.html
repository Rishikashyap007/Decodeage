Comprehending Biological Ageing

It is crucial to comprehend the idea of biological ageing in order to comprehend how Ca-AKG reduces biological age. In contrast to chronological age, which just shows the passage of time, biological age shows how an organism's tissues and cells are functioning. Numerous variables, such as oxidative stress, DNA damage, and cellular malfunction, all play a role in biological ageing and contribute to the progressive deterioration of physiological performance over time.
