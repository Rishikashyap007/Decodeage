What Sets Ca-AKG Apart from AAKG and What Makes AKG Different?

Discover the distinction between AKG and Ca-AKG, your favourite molecules, to determine which is the winner.

P

Written by Parth Amin

Updated over a week ago

OverviewThe body contains a substance called alpha-ketoglutarate (AKG), which is essential to the Krebs cycle as well as other metabolic and cellular processes. It is essential for several cellular processes as well as the synthesis of energy. various people are thinking about supplementing because of its various health benefits, especially in terms of longevity and ageing. AKG comes in a variety of forms, too, which can be perplexing for anyone trying to grasp its potential. The distinctions between AKG, Ca-AKG, and AAKG will be discussed in this article, along with the reasons Ca-AKG is the most useful type.

AKG: The Foundational Form

As previously indicated, the body uses AKG for a number of metabolic and cellular functions. It acts as a mediator in the Krebs cycle, which is essential for the synthesis of energy. However, standard alpha-ketoglutarate has limits when used as a stand-alone supplement. According to studies, up to 80% of AKG administered orally is not absorbed well and does not enter the bloodstream. This considerably lowers its effectiveness as a supplement.

AAKG: Levoarginine Attached to AKG

L-arginine, an amino acid, and AKG are combined to form AAKG, or arginine alpha-ketoglutarate. This formulation amplifies the effects of AKG on blood and hormones. Although AAKG is thought to be better than conventional AKG, it is still not as effective as Ca-AKG. The main uses of AAKG are to enhance blood flow and support sports performance.

Ca-AKG: AKG Is Bound to Calcium

Among AKG forms, Calcium Alpha-Ketoglutarate, or Ca-AKG, is the most potent. This substance forms a bond between excitatory calcium and AKG, which increases the stability and bioavailability of the supplement. Ca-AKG is employed in clinical research due to its ability to reverse biological ageing and improve heart and cell health. It is particularly well-known for its benefits related to lifespan. It is thought to suppress mTOR, a protein that controls cell growth and metabolism, while raising AMPK activity, an energy sensor that controls cellular metabolism.

Comparison Study of AKG Forms

There are other kinds of AKG supplements available, including Ca-AKG, AKG, and AAKG, which you may have observed while investigating AKG supplements. Let's examine these forms more closely to have a deeper understanding of their distinctions.

The ability to be bioavailable

A substance's ability to be absorbed and used by the body is referred to as its bioavailability. According to study, Ca-AKG has a better bioavailability than AKG and AAKG when it comes to AKG. This is because calcium has been added, which improves the body's ability to absorb and use AKG. Therefore, Ca-AKG can be your best option if you're seeking for an AKG supplement that is quickly absorbed and efficiently used.

Benefits of Anti-Aging and Longevity

Ca-AKG has been strongly associated with anti-aging and long life effects in a number of studies. In animal models, it has demonstrated encouraging outcomes in terms of increasing lifespan and enhancing HealthSpan. This is because the substance can boost mitochondrial function, which is important for longevity and ageing. Therefore, Ca-AKG might be something to think about if you're interested in adding an AKG supplement to your anti-aging regimen.

The makeup of molecules

Because calcium has been added, the molecular makeup of Ca-AKG (C5H6CaO6) is different from that of AKG (C5H6O5). As a consequence, the chemical becomes almost 97% AKG. Compared to other forms of AKG, Ca-AKG has stronger anti-aging properties and a higher bioavailability, which could be explained by its larger percentage of AKG.

In summary

To sum up, Ca-AKG is a better version of AKG because of its molecular makeup, longer half-life, and enhanced anti-aging effects. But each person's body is unique, so some people might react better to a different type of AKG. It's advisable to speak with a medical expert before beginning a new supplement regimen.
