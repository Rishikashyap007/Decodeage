For whom should Ca-AKG be avoided?

When calcium channel blockers are taken concurrently with Ca-AKG, there may be interactions that reduce the medication's capacity to effectively treat heart and blood pressure disorders. If you are using calcium channel blocks or already have renal problems, stay away from taking Ca-AKG. If you have a history of bleeding disorders, are taking blood thinners, are pregnant, or are nursing a baby, speak with your doctor before using this supplement.
