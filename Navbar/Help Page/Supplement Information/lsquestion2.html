Is LongeVit Allowed?

For most people, LongeVit is usually regarded as safe. But like any supplement, there are a few things to keep in mind:

Distinctions Among NMN Isomers

  • Health disorders: Before beginning LongeVit, it is imperative that you speak with a healthcare professional if you have any underlying medical disorders or kidney-related problems because it contains Ca-AKG.

Which is better for someone with kidney problems: NMN or LongeVit?

It is advised to choose NMN over LongeVit if a person with kidney problems is using calcium channel blockers, as LongeVit's Ca-AKG component may raise concerns.
