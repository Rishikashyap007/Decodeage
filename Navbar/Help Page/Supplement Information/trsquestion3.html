Who Should Avoid Resveratrol

1. Pregnant and Breastfeeding Women:

It is not advised for pregnant or nursing women to take resveratrol supplements.

2. People with Bleeding Disorders:

Due to possible interactions, people with bleeding disorders should use caution when taking resveratrol.

3. Individuals on Specific drugs:

Caution should be exercised by those taking anti-coagulant, anti-platelet, and high blood pressure drugs. Because of its anticoagulant and blood pressure-lowering properties, resveratrol may interact with certain medications. Additionally, it might impact liver treatments.

4.People with Hormone-Sensitive Conditions:

Women with breast cancer and people with a history of estrogen-dependent tumours, such as prostate cancer, should not take resveratrol since it can mimic the effects of oestrogen in the body.

5.Patients Both Before and After Surgery:

Because resveratrol has anticoagulant qualities that raise the risk of bleeding, it should be avoided two weeks prior to and following surgery.
