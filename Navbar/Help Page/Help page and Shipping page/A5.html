How Do I Get My Money Back for Returns at Decode Age?

Learn the steps for a hassle-free refund at Decode Age, processed via your original payment method.

Refund Eligibility and Request

To start the refund process, log into your account, choose the appropriate order, and then click "Request Refund." Make sure your item satisfies our return policy requirements, which include returning it in its original condition within the allotted time frame, before proceeding.

Payment Gateway Refunds

Since we know that receiving a refund can be annoying, we make every effort to make it simpler for you by returning the money to the identical payment method that you originally used. This keeps the return procedure uniform and simple to comprehend.

Refund Timing

Refunds are often handled seven to ten business days after the returned item is received. A confirmation email should be sent to you as soon as your monies are returned to you.
It's crucial to maintain a note of the details of your return and to keep an eye on the amount of your refund on your credit card bill. To start a refund inquiry if you do not see the refund there, get in touch with customer service.
In the event that you need assistance, we are always available. We can be reached by phone, email, or chat.
