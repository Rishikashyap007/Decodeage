Are There Shipping Fees for My Decode Age Order?


Are There Shipping Fees for My Decode Age Order?

Find out when shipping is free and when additional fees apply, especially for international customers.

P

Written by Parth Amin
Updated over a week ago


Shipping Information for India and International Orders

When you place an order at our store, shipping within India is free of charge. Yes, there aren't any shipping costs involved at all! Shipping charges for orders placed from overseas will be computed and applied to the total cost of your package.

Ground Shipping in India

You are qualified for free ground shipping on all orders placed within India. However, shipping costs will be incurred for orders placed from abroad and will depend on the package's weight, size, and final destination.

Shipping Fees For International Orders

It is significant to remember that shipping costs will apply to orders placed from outside of India. The package's weight, size, and ultimate destination will all affect the cost of international shipping.

How the Fees Are Calculated at Checkout

You will be able to see and calculate the shipping charges before completing the order during the checkout process. Customers will find it easy to shop with us because they can easily discover the precise cost of their package.

Conclusion

We trust that this post has addressed all of your inquiries concerning international and Indian shipping. Do not hesitate to get in touch with us if you have any more queries.
