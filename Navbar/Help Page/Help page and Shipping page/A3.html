What If My Decode Age Package is Missing?

Order delayed? Find out how to get fast help on your missing Decode Age delivery.

Missing Packages
When Should You Worry About Delivery?

You can anticipate prompt delivery when you place an order with DecodeAge. The size of your order and your location determine how long it will take to arrive. those placed from outside the United States will usually arrive in 5 business days, while those placed from outside may take up to 4 weeks.

How to Report a Missing Package

Please contact our customer service staff at care@decodeage.com if you think your package is missing or is taking longer than planned to arrive. Add your order number, the purchase date, and, if you can, any correspondence or tracking information. With this information, we can look into the problem and quickly find a solution.

Details You'll Need to Share

The more information you can give our customer support team when reporting a lost or apparently delayed package, the better. We recommend giving us your order number, the date of purchase, and any tracking information or communication you have received.
We appreciate your patience. Our customer support staff will make every effort to look into the matter and provide a prompt solution.
