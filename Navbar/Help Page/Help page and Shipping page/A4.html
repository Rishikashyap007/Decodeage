Changing Your Address Fast

You must update your address as soon as possible, ideally before your order is packed. Don't put off updating the information in your account until it's too late. Here's how to quickly change your address.

Quick Address Change Before Packing

Changes to your address are only available prior to the packing of your order. Take immediate action by going to your account, viewing your order data, and choosing 'Edit Address' to make the required adjustments.

Can't Change Address? Here's Why

The address is locked in and cannot be changed once your order status indicates "Packed" or "Shipped." For help in such situations, get in touch with customer care.

Conclusion

Only while the order is still in its early stages can you make changes to your address. Take advantage of this and update your information as soon as possible.
